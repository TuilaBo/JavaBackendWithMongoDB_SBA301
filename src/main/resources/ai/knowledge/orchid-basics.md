# Orchid Product Knowledge

Lan Ho Diep (Phalaenopsis) is a premium orchid commonly used for home decor and gifts in Vietnam.

Lan Ho Diep prefers indirect sunlight, stable humidity, and moderate watering. Overwatering can damage roots.

For product copywriting, highlight elegance, longevity of blooms, and suitability as gifts for business and family occasions.

Price positioning can be segmented by pot size, stem count, flower color rarity, and decorative package quality.

Suggested tone for e-commerce copy: concise, trustworthy, and premium-oriented; avoid exaggerated medical or false claims.
